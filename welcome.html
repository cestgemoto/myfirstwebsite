The Channel

I've been dabbling on and off of YouTube for years, but I've recently decided to hunker down and commit to growing my following! On my channel, you can find fun challenges that can boost your creativity, discussions on songwriting and music making, and explorations on learning a new language.

You can also find a new series of videos I've been trying to toy with for months now! Recall the Mall! is a lighthearted improv interview type of video where I interrogate unsuspecting victims at the local Governer's Square Mall in my hometown of Clarksville. Enjoy intense questions such as:

  • Would you kill baby Hitler?
  • Would you sneak into a concert without a ticket if you knew you wouldn't be cuaght?
  • Is it ethical to pirate music in the current era? Is it necessary?
  • Would you eat the last slice of pizza if no one was looking?

and more dumb questions with potentially interesting answers!

The Programming

In 2015, I had a major open-heart surgery that left me unable to leave my bed for days. It was being on my computer and phone that kept me sane, and the rush of excitement when I leanred how to make a button for a website interactive has stuck with me ever since! Learning a language to solve a problem is like what my brain was built to do!

Since then, I've been programming on and off - mostly off - for the past 8 years. It's my hope that this excercise re-acquaints me with front-end design so I can finally begin building my back-end reportoire.
